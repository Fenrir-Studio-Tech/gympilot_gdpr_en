Privacy Policy


  1. Introduction and Scope of the Policy


    This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "Policy") explains how Gympilot collects, uses, discloses, and protects the personal information of Users. This Policy applies to all information collected by Gympilot during the use of the application by Users.

  2. Collection and Use of Personal Data


    We collect various personal information when you use Gympilot. This includes:


    • Identification Information: Name, first name, email address, and phone number (if applicable).

    • Biometric Data: Height, weight, date of birth, and other health and fitness-related information.

    • Usage Data: Information on how you use Gympilot, including training choices.

    • Technical Data: Information about the device used to access the application, including device type, operating system, IP address, and connection information.

    • Payment Information: Payment details necessary to process subscription transactions.


      This information is used to:


    • Provide and improve our services, including training programs and meal plans.

    • Communicate with you, including sending notifications, updates, and promotional information related to our services.

    • Conduct statistical and market analysis to improve the application.

    • Ensure security and protection against fraud.


  3. Data Sharing and Disclosure


    Gympilot is committed not to sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

    • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us provide and improve our services, such as payment processing companies, cloud service providers, and marketing service providers.

    • Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose your information if it is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, to protect the rights, property, or safety of Gympilot, our Users, or others.

    • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred. You will be informed of any change of ownership or use of your personal information.

  4. Data Security


    We take the security of your personal data very seriously. We employ technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. This includes the use of encryption technologies to secure data during transmission and storage. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or data storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

  5. User Rights over Their Data


    As a Gympilot User, you have certain rights regarding your personal data. You have the right to access your data, request its correction or update, and request its deletion. You can exercise these rights by contacting Gympilot via the email address

    contact@fenrirstudio.io. The deletion of your personal data will result in the termination of your account and the cessation of access to Gympilot services.

  6. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies


    Currently, Gympilot does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect information about Users. However, this may change in the future to improve our services. In such a case, we will update this Policy and inform Users of the changes. Users will have the option to consent or refuse the use of these technologies.

  7. Changes to the Privacy Policy


    We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon publication on the Gympilot application or website. We encourage you to review this Policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.

  8. Contact and Communication


If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your rights related to your personal data, please contact Gympilot via the email address contact@fenrirstudio.io. We are committed to responding to all requests regarding the protection of your data as quickly as possible.
